Global Guardrails

Applied to every row, regardless of market, right after Gemini's raw output
🤔 Confidence is Gemini's certainty, not correctness — it can be 90% sure and still make the same mistake every time (mouthing-along looking like Dance, a track titled "Dance" nudging a lyric video the wrong way). That's exactly what these catch.
🕺
Dance vs Lip Sync
Dance cuesChoreography, body movement, synced routine
Lip Sync cuesFace performance, mouthing lyrics, minimal body movement
ResolvesMotion-type confusion between the two most-confused categories
🎤
Lyrics Protection
ProblemSong title contains "Dance" → static lyric video mistagged as Dance
Cues checkedVisible lyrics, lyrics page, Spotify lyrics, text-focused content
FixRe-tag as Lyrics when cues are present
🖼️
Carousel Detection
TriggerisSlideshow = True, or TikTok photo post
Action"Carousel" tag automatically included
Why it mattersPrevents slideshow posts being force-fit into video-only categories

Creative Knowledge Base (KB)

Sits between global and market guardrails — supplies historical pattern context, not row-level memory
1
Gemini sees the content and produces a raw tag
↓
2
KB checks this creator / track's historical tagging pattern, if one exists
↓
3
Guardrails use that pattern to decide the final label
🔑 Keyed by identity — not similarity
CreatorThis account has posted 40 videos before — 35 of them ended up as Dance
Track / SoundThis exact audio has appeared across many rows — usually resolves to Lip Sync in PH, but Dance in KR
Looks up "have we seen this creator/track before," never "does this look like some other video." No visual similarity search — only exact creator or track matches count.
🌱
New creator or new track, never seen before? KB has no history to check, so it contributes nothing for that row — Gemini's output goes straight to Global and Market guardrails on its own. Not a failure, just an empty lookup. It resolves itself as soon as a few rows exist for that creator/track.
